La Lagunita Duck Lodge

Uruguay Safaris

La Lagunita lodge, is located in Chuy, Rocha, the best hunting area of duck in Uruguay. We offer a complete duck program every morning, where you will hunt species such as Rosy-Bill Pochards, Yellow-billed pintail and Siriri pampa. In the afternoons you can choose to hunt some duck, dove or perdiz. La Lagunita is located 3 hours and a half from the Montevideo Airport. The lodge itself is a beautiful architecture that has large common areas with a great fireplace, barbecue with Uruguayan grill and individual rooms.
