Santa Cristina Perdiz Lodge

Uruguay Safaris

We are very happy to offer you our new program of Mixed bag, in Santa Cristina, located in the department of Durazno, the best area of perdiz in Uruguay. Santa Cristina is next to several large dove roosts and great pasture lands for an excellent perdiz. Throughout this adventure, you will hunt perdiz behind well trained Brittanys in the morning, and afternoons of shooting dove. Also now you can opt to include one morning of duck hunting. Santa Cristina is an elegant place located some 125 miles northwest from Montevideo. Getting there is easy, as you will arrive in Montevideo where one of us will greet you and transfer you about two and a half hours to Santa Cristina. We have really nice facilities and all rooms are single with king beds.
